Donate Your Vehicle

Donate your car, truck, SUV, van, motorcycle, trailer, or boat to NAWS and make a real difference in the lives of animals.

Why Donate?

  • Get a lawful IRS tax deduction for the fair market value
  • Avoid DMV paperwork and vehicle listing headaches
  • Free pickup—running or not (some restrictions apply)
  • Help fund NAWS services for pets in need
